ABOUT ME


Hello!! Thank you for taking time to know about me.
My names is Jackline Wanjiru Njoroge and i am a woman in technology .I was born in the early 2000's as a fourth born in a family of five.My parents Ephantus Njoroge Kibere and Sarah Kiarie welcomed me with love and named me Jackline Wanjiru Njoroge and for sure at no point have i ever sat down to question or regret about my names.

Life had to continue and so i grew up in Nairobi's Kasarani area, where i experienced childhood all the way till 2010 where i joined a boarding school in Murang'a and got to experience a change in environment. Fast foward i finished my primary school in 2013 and attained 372 mark in my KCPE that saw me join Kahuhia Girls in 2013.

Four years ended in 2017 and i left Kahuhia Girls with a C+ then joined Regional Center for Mapping of Resources which is located a long Thika in Roysambu. Despite the pandemic i managed to go through and complete my two years pursuing a KNEC Diploma in Information Communication Technology and passed with a Credit. I was now out in school and now did not know what to do or where to begin from. So i decided to take a two months break to just relax and make use of my hobbies such as cooking sweet meals. During this time word reached to me that Moringa School was offering scholarships for 100 women willing to become Women in Tech. I was skeptical because i was wondering, "will i be among the selected few?". BY God's grace or as the goes 'Mungu ni Nani'...... i was selected... like i was picked to be a woman in technology. This is how on February 15th i begun my journey with Moringa School to be nurtured to become a Software Developer.

I didn't mention but in the beginning of 2019 i started my career in Social Media Marketing with wowzi Technologies and so, with all that workload ihave to plan every of my day so that i can get to accomodate and balance all aspects of my life.
